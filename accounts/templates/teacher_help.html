Help & Support

Guide on how to use the Teacher Portal effectively.

Getting Started

1

Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is the central hub. Here you can see your weekly schedule, active classes, and quick stats about your attendance.

2

Checking Your Schedule

Use the tabs on the dashboard (Mon, Tue, Wed, etc.) to view your classes for specific days. Today's classes are highlighted.

Managing Attendance

1

Daily Check-in

Click the "Mark Attendance" button on the dashboard top right corner. You'll need to scan your face to verify your identity and mark your presence for the day.

2

Starting a Class

When it's time for a class, locate it in your schedule table and click the green "Start Class" button. This initiates the session tracking.

3

Live Monitoring

During a class, keep the "Live Class Monitoring" tab open. The system periodically verifies your presence via camera to track active duration.

4

Ending a Class

Once your class is finished, click the red "End Class" button. This finalizes the session and logs the total active duration.

Viewing Records

1

Previous Records

Navigate to the "Previous Records" page from the sidebar to view a complete history of your completed classes, including dates, times, and adherence scores.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my face isn't recognized?
Ensure you are in a well-lit area and remove mask/glasses. If the problem persists, contact the principal to re-register your face data.
Can I edit my timetable?
No, timetables are managed by the school administration (Principal). Please request any schedule changes through them.
How is my "Active Duration" calculated?
The system checks your presence in front of the camera every few seconds while a class is ongoing. Your active duration is the sum of time segments where you were successfully verified.
